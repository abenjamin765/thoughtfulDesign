The course teaches a practice: seeing the system beneath the screen, understanding
the people a design affects, and accounting for the consequences of every choice.
This blog is where that practice meets the week's real work — the messy decisions,
the trade-offs, and the questions that don't have a single correct answer.

Expect short, concrete pieces: a pattern worth stealing, a failure worth studying,
a method worth questioning. Nothing here replaces the lessons. It's the thinking
in between.

If a post resonates, the curriculum goes deeper. Start with Module 1 — *From
Screens to Systems* — and the rest will have more to push against.
