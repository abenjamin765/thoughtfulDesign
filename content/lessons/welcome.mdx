## Why objects first?

Most digital products are built feature-first. OOUX flips that: start with the **objects** in a user's mental model, then design relationships, actions, and attributes around them.

That shift helps teams:

- Break down complex systems without losing the big picture
- Align design, content, and engineering on shared structure
- Catch structural problems before pixels get polished

<Callout type="tip">

OOUX is not about object-oriented programming. It is about designing for how humans naturally perceive **things** in the world.

</Callout>
